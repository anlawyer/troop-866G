SCOUT ME IN

Troop 866G is a Scouts BSA Girl Troop located in Imperial Beach, California.

As a group of motivated girls and young women, we live the values of Scouting and strive for excellence in all we do.

  • There are over 135 merit badges that Scouts can earn, including Camping, Emergency Preparedness, Journaling, and Environmental Science.
  • There are 7 ranks that Scouts can advance through, including the prestigious Eagle Scout rank.
  • We work on attaining these achievements during weekly meetings and monthly outdoor events.

Interested in joining us?

We meet every Monday at 6:15PM at St. James Lutheran Church, 866 Imperial Beach Blvd.
